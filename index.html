About us

FPCI Chapter UIN Jakarta, established in 2020, is a student community based in UIN Jakarta as a university-level chapter of FPCI with direct subordinate relation to FPCI. Therefore, FPCI Chapter UIN Jakarta shares the same goals as FPCI, which is to spread Indonesia internationalism. FPCI Chapter UIN Jakarta in the Board of 25.26 brings the vision with a reference to the concept of "Syi'ar" in Islam, which emphasizes the visible expression and dissemination of faith, values, and identity.
